Progression Rules
Don't skip steps — each one builds the foundation for the next.
A step is "done" when it feels easy, not just achievable.
Work on 2–3 progressions simultaneously — variety accelerates neural adaptation.
When you plateau, drop back one step and nail it harder before advancing.

Timing Protocol
Pre-workout (45–90 min): 30–40g carbs + 20g protein.
Intra (60+ min sessions only): 20–30g fast carbs — banana, dates.
Post (within 1 hr): 40g+ protein + 40–60g carbs.
Before bed: 20–30g casein (cottage cheese / Greek yogurt).

Lifestyle System
Habits that compound over weeks
▼
💤
Sleep
Averaging 8 hours — excellent. At 17, HGH is secreted almost entirely during deep sleep. Dark, cold room. No screens 30 min before. Magnesium glycinate before bed deepens quality.
🌊
Liquid Motion (Daily)
1–2 rounds every day. Morning to wake joints, post-workout as cooldown.

30 sec each: Prayer Squat → Shoulder Dislocates → Crab Reach → Elephant Walks → V-W Stretch → Roundhouse Kick Stretch → Hip Flexor → Lateral Gorilla Walk
⚡
Hourly Movement
Every sedentary hour: 10 air squats, 10 calf raises, 30-sec dead hang, or 5 push-ups. Circulation management, not exercise. Especially critical in Texas summer.
🧘
Zhan Zhuang
5–10 min daily, 15 on Thursdays. Postural integrity, proprioception, body awareness. Barefoot on grass when possible.
💧
Hydration
Aim for 3–4 liters daily. Add electrolytes after intense sessions. 2% dehydration = measurable performance decline.
🧠
The Slump Mindset
You're reactivating, not starting over. Weeks 1–3 feel slow. Week 4+ the curve steepens. Log PRs weekly, not daily.
Week 1–4 Comeback Adjustments
Weeks 1–2: 60–70% intensity. Joints need more adaptation time than muscles.
Week 3: restore full volume. Note where you're stronger than expected.
Week 4: test all benchmarks.
Joint soreness = normal. Sharp localized pain = stop immediately.

12-Week Comeback Roadmap
Wk 1–2
Reactivation
60–70% intensity. Form focus. Start creatine. No complex skill work.
Wk 3–4
Volume Restoration
Full volume returns. Retest benchmarks end of week 4. Add weighted pulls and dips.
Wk 5–8
Strength Push
Progressive overload every 2 weeks. Advanced tuck front lever. 20+ min handstand sessions.
Wk 9–10
Deload & Integration
Reduce volume 30%. Prioritize skill quality. Let adaptations consolidate.
Wk 11–12
Peak & Test
Max effort on all benchmarks. First backflip on soft mat. Document everything.
